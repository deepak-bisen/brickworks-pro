Our Process

Our commitment to quality begins with our process. We combine traditional techniques with modern standards to ensure every brick is perfect.

1

Sourcing Materials

We select the finest quality soil and sand from trusted local sources, ensuring the base material is pure and free from contaminants.

2

Mixing & Blending

Our precise mixture ratios guarantee consistent strength and texture. Water and clay are blended to the perfect consistency.

3

Molding & Pressing

State-of-the-art machinery molds and compresses the mixture into uniform shapes with sharp edges.

4

Drying Process

Bricks are carefully air-dried in controlled environments to prevent cracks.

5

Firing Process

Bricks are then fired in our high-temperature kiln, hardening them to last a lifetime.
